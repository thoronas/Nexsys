Frequently asked
questions

Does NEXSYS Financial fund or sell mortgages?

NEXSYS does not sell mortgages. We simply assist Agents and represent a mortgage application to a Lender on behalf of the originating Agent until the deal is funded. After funding, deals are co-brokered back to Agents through Filogix, as these deals belong to the originating Agent.

Who is currently using the NEXSYS Advantage?

Brokers throughout Canada, from Super Brokers to Independents, are enjoying the benefits that come from NEXSYS.

Can I speak to a live representative about this program?

Yes, call 877-901-4997 x206 to speak to our Business Development Manager or email us at sales@nexsysfinancial.com.

How do Lenders recognize NEXSYS Advantage deals?

When NEXSYS submits deals to a Lender, the Lender always sees the name of the originating Agent and their respective Brokerage so they understand where deals are ultimately coming from. Having said this, it is NEXSYS that recognizes and rewards volumes submitted by a Brokerage.

How much does this service cost for brokerages?

There is a one-time $750 cost to register your brokerage with NEXSYS and Agents pay an annual $50 registration fee. The service is free for Agents who submit sufficient deals with healthy funding ratios. For those that are unable to do so, there are reasonable submission and funded deals costs. Details »
