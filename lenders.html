Brokerage/Agent
Verification & Registration

This preliminary review determines if a registrant meets a Lender's basic guidelines. The process includes:

  • Checks of Provincial Regulatory Bodies
  • Key strategic alliance with Equifax utilizing products such as E-IDverifier
  • Registration of Brokerage, Brokerage Owners, Principal Broker, and Agents
  • Increased scrutiny (AML Compliance)

Underwriter
Deal Screening

This preliminary review determines if an application meets a Lender's basic guidelines. The process includes:

  • Receipt of application
  • Creation of unique customer file
  • Review for critical missing information
  • Income review
  • Credit bureau review
  • Application assessment

Underwriter Review

This comprehensive review of the deal, including confirmation of income, includes:

  • Underwriter reviews all documents and confirms data.
  • Underwriter reviews employment letter and phones employer or relevant parties to confirm the authenticity of income.

Income Verification

An Underwriter confirms stated income by carefully reviewing the following:

  • T1 General
  • Letter of Employment
  • Call to Employer or Accountant
  • Notice of Assessment
  • Pay Stub
  • Business License
  • Articles of Incorporation
  • Financial Statements
  • Bank Statements
  • Pension Statements
  • Investments
  • Stated Income Declaration

Frequently Asked Questions

Does NEXSYS Financial fund or sell mortgages?

NEXSYS does not sell mortgages. We simply assist Agents and represent a mortgage application to a Lender on behalf of the originating Agent until the deal is funded. After funding, deals are co-brokered back to Agents through Filogix, as these deals belong to the originating Agent.
