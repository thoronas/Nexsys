How does it work?

Once you have registered, you receive a unique login and may enjoy secure access the NEXSYS site as required. When a mortgage application is submitted, you receive immediate email confirmation letting you know what pre-submission documents are required.
As each document is collected, you upload, email or fax the documents to the NEXSYS site and each document is converted into an electronic format and stored for future referance. Agents and Brokers can keep track of the progress of each deal throughout the process to funding.
With NEXSYS Advantage Plus, the team will even collect all related documentation from your customer, on your behalf. All documents uploaded to NEXSYS can be quickly downloaded at any time.
The end result is a lender-complete electronic file that the Broker may use as part of their provincially compliant file.

Any Benefits?

A seasoned team meticulously scrutinizes deals, prepares mortgage related documents for signing, orders appraisals, and ensures deals close as planned.
Lenders receive quality deals; enjoy increased funding ratios and the benefit of receiving verified applications, thereby reducing the risk of fraud.
The NEXSYS process is Lender supported and dedicated solely to the certification and validation of mortgage applications submitted to Lenders.
